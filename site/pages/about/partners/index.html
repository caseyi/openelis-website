Partners & supporters

OpenELIS Global is sustained by a coalition of public institutions, funders, and country implementers committed to open laboratory infrastructure.

Stewarded by

The Digital Initiatives Group at the University of Washington's Department of Global Health leads OpenELIS development and community stewardship.

Recognized as a global good

Funders & partners

OpenELIS is developed with support from public-health institutions and in collaboration with a global network of partners.

Deployments worldwide

OpenELIS powers national laboratory networks and clinical labs across Africa, the Caribbean, Asia, and the Pacific — including Côte d'Ivoire, Haiti, Mauritius, Indonesia, Ethiopia, Madagascar, Papua New Guinea, Vietnam, Tanzania, Lesotho, Nepal, and Cambodia, with additional deployments in the Democratic Republic of Congo, Liberia, India, and South Sudan.
