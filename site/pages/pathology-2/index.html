Why it matters

Pathology and cytology are collaborative, multi-stage disciplines — a case moves through grossing, processing, staining, screening, and sign-off, often across several people. Running that on paper or a separate system fragments the record. OpenELIS keeps the whole case, and its link to the rest of the patient's lab work, in one place.

Built for the pathology workflow

Stage-based case progression

Cases advance through defined stages with clear status at every step — technician and pathologist assignments visible on the worklist.

Cytology & IHC

Dedicated cytology screening and immunohistochemistry scoring workflows, alongside anatomic pathology reporting.

Multi-user collaboration

Technicians, screeners, and pathologists work the same case with role-based visibility and a shared, auditable record.

Connected to the whole lab

Pathology results live beside the patient's other tests and flow to EMRs and reporting through the same interoperability layer.
