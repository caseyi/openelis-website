Catalyst: an AI assistant for your lab data

Ask for a report in plain language and get it — no SQL, no waiting for IT. Catalyst is an AI-powered data assistant in active development for OpenELIS, built privacy-first: the model sees your database schema, never patient data.

In active development — phased release from 2026

Why it matters

Custom reports are one of the biggest bottlenecks in laboratory operations — lab managers wait on IT, or learn SQL, or go without. Catalyst puts reporting back in the hands of the people who know the lab, using natural language, while keeping patient data completely private.

Three capabilities in development

Data assistant

Generate custom reports and dashboard widgets from natural-language prompts. "Monthly HIV viral load summary by facility" returns exactly that — chat mode, wizard mode, and scheduling.

Context-aware help

Press for help from any screen and get answers based on your current page and role — natural-language questions, video tutorials, and guided walkthroughs.

Visual form builder

Design custom order-entry screens by drag-and-drop instead of editing configuration by hand — describe what you need and build compliant forms.

Privacy-first by design

The AI only ever sees your database schema — never patient data. Queries are generated by the model, but results are fetched and displayed entirely within OpenELIS, on your infrastructure. Deploy the language model locally, on a shared Ministry server, or via a cloud API — your choice — and when it's unavailable, a traditional query builder keeps reporting working.
